383

The King of 60s Pickups

In 1967, Dodge introduced their 383 (6.3L) engine to the Sweptline family. At the time, it was the most powerful engine offered in any full-size pickup at 325 hp and 425 lb-ft of torque. These days, it's a highly sought-after powerplant for many classic Mopar restos.
